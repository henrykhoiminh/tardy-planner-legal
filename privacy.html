Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Summary: Tardy Planner collects the information you provide (your account details from Google or Apple sign-in, the people ("Members") you add to track tardiness, parties, events, and check-in results) and some automatic data (usage analytics, device info, crash reports) to operate and improve the app. We do not sell your personal information. You can delete your account and data at any time.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tardy Planner. This Privacy Policy explains how Henry Tran ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the Tardy Planner mobile application ("App") and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Information: Tardy Planner only supports signing in with Google or Apple. When you sign in, we receive your name and email address from the identity provider and use them to create and manage your account. You may choose to provide additional profile information such as a display name or avatar.

Member, Party, and Event Data: To provide the core service, we collect the information you enter about the people you track ("Members"), including the names or nicknames you assign them and the default tardiness offsets you configure. We also collect the parties (groups of Members) and events (planned times, descriptions, and modifiers such as "vacation," "weather," "morning," or "formal") that you create.

Check-In and Arrival Data: When you record whether a Member arrived on time, late, or not at all, we store that result and use it to update the Member's tardiness offset and the party's tardy score.

Shared Content: When you generate a shareable screenshot (e.g., a "Roast Mode," "Gentle Nudge," or "Formal Shade" card) and share it outside the App, the content you share (including any Member names or nicknames you chose to display) leaves our control and is handled by whatever platform you share it to.

Communications: If you contact us for support or feedback, we collect the content of your messages along with your contact information.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage and Analytics Data: We collect information about how you interact with the Service, including features used, screens viewed, events created, and actions taken. We use this data to understand engagement and improve the App.

Device Information: We collect device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, app version, language, and time zone.

Log and Crash Data: Our systems automatically record information when you use the Service, including IP address, access times, and app crashes or errors.

Push Notification Tokens: If you enable push notifications, we store the push token issued by your device so we can send you check-in reminders (for example, at T-60, T-0, and T+15 relative to an event).

2.3 Information About Other People

Tardy Planner is designed for you to track people you know (friends, family, coworkers). You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to input information about those Members. We recommend using first names, nicknames, or initials rather than full personal details. We do not attempt to identify, contact, or build profiles on Members who are not themselves users of the Service.

2.4 Information from Third Parties

Because sign-in is handled exclusively through Apple or Google, we receive basic profile information from those providers (typically name and email) according to your privacy settings with that provider. We may also receive subscription status information from our payment processor when you subscribe to Tardy Planner Pro.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

Provide and maintain the Service: Create and manage your account, generate arrival predictions from your parties and events, send you check-in notifications, update tardy scores, and render shareable screenshots.

Improve and develop the Service: Analyze usage patterns, diagnose crashes, and develop new features.

Communicate with you: Send service-related announcements, respond to inquiries, and provide customer support.

Personalize your experience: Tailor predictions and content to how you use the App.

Process subscriptions and show ads: If you subscribe to Tardy Planner Pro, we use payment information (handled by our payment processor and the applicable app store) to process the transaction. If you use the free tier, we may display banner advertisements; ad providers may collect limited device identifiers as described in their own privacy policies.

Ensure safety and security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
